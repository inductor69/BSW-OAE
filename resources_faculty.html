For Faculty

In most cases, students with learning disabilities are not particularly identified, which results in them being neglected or are unable to perform to their very best. One of the major causes for this is the lack of awareness among faculties and the school authorities. They usually end up tagging the students as slow learners or incapable. We make our efforts to rectify this by organizing workshops to create awareness and sensitivity towards such students, which also enables them to identify the problem and intervene at the right time. With more digitalization and virtual classrooms, more issues have come up, and the need to address them has never been greater. These workshops aim to ensure that the students in need are to be taken into consideration while taking lectures and providing an environment where they too can bloom.
